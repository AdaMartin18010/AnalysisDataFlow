# Document Template

> **Language**: English | **Last Updated**: 2026-04-21

Use this template as a starting point for new English-language documents.

## Required Sections

1. **Definitions** — At least one `Def-*` numbered definition
2. **Properties** — At least one `Lemma-*` or `Prop-*`
3. **Relations** — Links to related concepts/systems
4. **Argumentation** — Boundary analysis, counterexamples
5. **Proof / Engineering Argument** — Complete proof or rigorous reasoning
6. **Examples** — Code snippets, configurations, cases
7. **Visualizations** — At least one Mermaid diagram
8. **References** — `[^n]` format, centralized

## Naming

Format: `{topic-keywords}.md` (all lowercase, hyphen-separated)
