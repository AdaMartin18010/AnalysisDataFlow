# AnalysisDataFlow 学术顾问回复处理流程

> **版本**: v1.0 | **创建日期**: 2026-04-12 | **状态**: 生效中 | **适用范围**: 第一批5位核心专家

---

## 🔄 流程概览

```
收到回复
    │
    ├──► 接受邀请 ──────► 启动入职流程
    │
    ├──► 婉拒 ──────────► 感谢记录，保持联系
    │
    ├──► 询问详情 ──────► 提供补充信息，安排通话
    │
    └──► 需要更多时间 ──► 设定跟进日期，定期联系
```

---

## 📋 回复分类与处理

### 分类标准

| 类别 | 定义 | 关键词 | 处理优先级 |
|------|------|--------|-----------|
| 接受 | 明确同意加入 | "accept", "join", "honored", "delighted" | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| 婉拒 | 明确拒绝 | "unable", "decline", "cannot", "too busy" | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| 询问 | 需要更多信息 | "questions", "more information", "details" | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| 待定 | 需要时间考虑 | "consider", "think about", "discuss" | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| 转介 | 推荐他人 | "refer", "colleague", "suggest" | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

### Step 3: 安排首次通话

**通话议程 (30分钟)**:

| 时间 | 主题 | 负责人 |
|------|------|--------|

- 网络拓展建议

## ❌ 婉拒处理

### 处理原则

1. **保持尊重**: 理解并尊重对方的时间安排
2. **表达感谢**: 感谢对方考虑邀请
3. **留下后路**: 保持联系，未来可能有机会
4. **请求反馈**: 询问是否可以提供改进建议

### 回复模板

```
Subject: Re: Invitation to Join AnalysisDataFlow Advisory Board — Thank You

Dear [Name],

Thank you for taking the time to consider our invitation and for your candid response.

We completely understand that you have many demands on your time, and we respect
your decision. Your contributions to the field through [具体成就] continue to be
an inspiration to our work.

If circumstances change in the future, we would be honored to revisit this invitation.
In the meantime, we hope to stay connected and will keep you updated on major
project milestones.

If you have any feedback on our project or this outreach process, we would be
grateful to hear it.

Thank you again for your consideration, and we wish you continued success in
your endeavors.

Warm regards,

[Your Name]
AnalysisDataFlow Project Team
```

### 后续行动

- [ ] 标记状态为"已婉拒"

- [ ] 6个月后发送项目进展更新（保持联系）
- [ ] 如有重大里程碑，发送特别通知

## ❓ 询问详情回复

### 常见问题与回复

#### Q1: 具体的时间承诺是什么？

**回复要点**:

- 年度全体会议：2-3小时（1次）
- 季度同步：1小时（可选，4次）
- 内容审阅：1-2小时（按需，2-3次/年）
- 总计：约15小时/年
- 大部分工作可异步完成

**回复模板**:

```
The time commitment is designed to be manageable:

- Annual Board Meeting: 2-3 hours (once per year)
- Quarterly Sync Calls: 1 hour each (optional, 4x per year)
- Content Reviews: 1-2 hours each (2-3x per year, as needed)
- **Total: approximately 15 hours per year**

Most engagement is asynchronous through email and collaborative documents.
We value your time and will always provide materials well in advance of any meeting.
```

#### Q2: 顾问委员会的具体职责是什么？

**回复要点**:

- 战略指导：审阅技术路线图
- 内容审核：形式化验证、技术准确性
- 学术背书：允许列名展示
- 网络拓展：引荐其他专家

**回复模板**:

```
The Advisory Board's responsibilities include:

1. **Strategic Guidance** (Annual): Review and advise on our technical roadmap
   and research priorities

2. **Content Review** (Quarterly/As needed): Provide expert feedback on:
   - Formalization of stream processing theory
   - Technical accuracy of engine documentation
   - Assessment of new technologies for inclusion

3. **Academic Endorsement**:
   - Listed on our website and documentation as an Academic Advisor
   - Optional participation in major release announcements

4. **Network Expansion**:
   - Suggest other experts who might be interested
   - Help connect us with academic institutions or industry labs

All participation is voluntary within these categories — we understand your
time is valuable and appreciate any level of engagement you can provide.
```

#### Q3: 项目的影响力/用户规模如何？

**回复要点**:

- 1,000+ 文档页面
- 10,000+ 形式化元素
- GitHub Stars / Forks 数据
- 社区活跃度指标

**回复模板**:

```
AnalysisDataFlow has grown to become:

📊 **Scale**:
- 1,000+ documentation pages
- 10,000+ formalized theorems, definitions, and proofs
- Comprehensive cross-reference system with dependency graphs
- 382 Flink-specific technical documents

🌐 **Reach**:
- [GitHub Stars] stars on GitHub
- [GitHub Forks] forks
- Active community discussions
- Referenced in academic courses and industry training

📈 **Growth**:
- Continuous updates (100% completion achieved in April 2026)
- Expanding into AI-driven streaming and real-time ML
- Multi-language support (English + Chinese)

We aim to become the definitive reference for stream processing theory and practice.
```

#### Q4: 是否有财务补偿？

**回复要点**:

- 目前为荣誉性质职位
- 无直接财务补偿
- 提供学术认可和网络机会
- 未来可能申请资助提供津贴

**回复模板**:

```
Currently, the Advisory Board is an honorary position without direct financial
compensation. This is typical for academic advisory roles in open-source
knowledge projects.

What we offer instead:
- Official recognition and listing as an Academic Advisor
- Formal certificate of appointment
- Early access to research findings and project developments
- Connection with other leading experts in the field
- Platform for influencing the direction of stream processing education

We are exploring grant opportunities that might allow us to offer modest
honoraria in the future, but this is not currently guaranteed.
```

## ⏳ 需要更多时间处理

### 处理策略

1. **表达理解**: 表示理解需要时间考虑
2. **提供支持**: 提供任何需要的额外信息
3. **设定时间框**: 建议一个合理的考虑期限
4. **定期跟进**: 在约定时间前温和提醒

### 回复模板

```
Subject: Re: Invitation to Join AnalysisDataFlow Advisory Board — Take Your Time

Dear [Name],

Thank you for your thoughtful response. We completely understand that this is
an important decision that deserves careful consideration.

Please take the time you need. To help with your decision, I'm happy to:
- Provide any additional information you might need
- Arrange a brief call to discuss any questions
- Connect you with any current advisors (once we have some) for their perspective

Would it be helpful if I follow up in [2 weeks]? Or feel free to reach out
whenever you're ready.

In the meantime, I've attached our complete project roadmap for your reference.

Thank you again for considering this opportunity.

Warm regards,

[Your Name]
AnalysisDataFlow Project Team
```

### 跟进时间表

| 时间节点 | 动作 | 内容 |
|----------|------|------|
| 初始回复 | 确认收到 | 表达理解，提供支持 |
| 1周后 | 温和提醒 | "Hope you're doing well. Just wanted to check if you need any additional information..." |
| 2周后 | 设定决定期限 | "Would you like more time, or would a call help?" |
| 1个月后 | 最终跟进 | "We understand if the timing isn't right. We hope to stay connected..." |

## 🔁 转介处理

### 处理流程

```
收到转介建议
    │
    ├──► 感谢转介
    │
    ├──► 了解被推荐人背景
    │       └── 是否适合当前批次？
    │
    ├──► 如果适合
    │       ├── 准备个性化邀请
    │       ├── 提及推荐人（征得同意）
    │       └── 加入下一批发送
    │
    └──► 如果不适合当前批次
            └── 记录到候选名单，未来联系
```

### 回复模板

```
Subject: Re: Invitation to Join AnalysisDataFlow Advisory Board — Thank You for the Referral

Dear [Name],

Thank you for your response and for suggesting [Referred Person] as a potential
advisor. We truly appreciate your help in identifying qualified candidates.

I would be delighted to reach out to [Referred Person]. Would you be comfortable
with me mentioning that you suggested they might be a good fit? Or would you
prefer to make an introduction yourself?

[If declining]: We completely understand your current commitments, and we hope
to have the opportunity to work together in the future. Thank you again for
your referral and for considering our invitation.

[If open to future]: We'll keep you updated on our progress, and perhaps we can
revisit this opportunity when your schedule allows.

Warm regards,

[Your Name]
AnalysisDataFlow Project Team
```
